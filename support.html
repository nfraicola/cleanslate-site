1) Getting started

How do I create an account?

Download CleanSlate from the App Store and sign up with your phone number.

What devices are supported?

iPhone running the iOS version listed on our App Store page. We aim to support the latest two iOS releases.

Where can I find my cards and connections?

Open the Cards tab to see all cards. Manage linked institutions under Manage Connections.

2) Cards & connections (Plaid)

How do I link a card or bank account?

Use Link Credit Card on the Cards tab or in Manage Connections. We connect securely via Plaid.

How do I unlink?

Open Manage Connections, select the institution, and slide left to unlink. You can also revoke access in your bank’s “Connected Apps”.

What data does CleanSlate access?

Read-only data such as account name/type, masked identifiers, balances, and transactions (amount, date, merchant, category) so we can display insights. We never receive your bank login.

I can’t see balances in Manage Connections or all my cards in the overview.

First, check your subscription status. The free tier supports up to 2 linked credit cards. To view balances and connect more than 2 cards, please subscribe (monthly or annual). If you’re subscribed and still don’t see balances or all cards, refresh the screen, try unlink/re-link, and then reach out to Support.

My new card isn’t showing after linking.

Pull to refresh on the Cards screen. If it still doesn’t appear, force-close and reopen the app, or unlink and re-link the institution.

3) Manually tracked cards

What are manually tracked cards?

Cards you add yourself (not linked via Plaid). You can set a nickname, last four digits, balance, APR, and minimum payment due.

What information is stored?

Only what you provide (e.g., nickname, last-4, balance, APR, minimum payment). This is not connected to any financial institution login.

How do I delete or export this data?

You can delete a manually tracked card in-app at any time. To request a copy of your data, contact us via the options in the Contact section.

4) Privacy & data

Do you store my bank login or full card numbers?

No. Connections are handled by Plaid. We do not receive or store your banking credentials.

Can I request a copy or deletion of my data?

Yes. Email us at privacy@cleanslate.app from the email associated with your account. Manually tracked cards can also be deleted directly in-app.

Where is your privacy policy?

See our full policy here: https://nfraicola.github.io/cleanslate-site/privacy.html

Tip: You can manage analytics/crash sharing from your device settings (where available).

5) Troubleshooting

Sync or linking issues

  • Pull to refresh on the Cards screen.
  • Verify your bank login works outside the app; watch for 2FA prompts.
  • Unlink and re-link the institution if needed.

Missing balances or cards

  • Confirm you’re on an active monthly or annual subscription.
  • The free tier supports up to 2 linked credit cards—additional cards require a subscription.
  • Refresh the screen, or unlink and re-link the institution.
  • If the issue persists, contact Support.

App crashes or freezes

  • Update to the latest iOS and CleanSlate version.
  • Force close and reopen the app.
  • Restart your device.

Wrong or missing transactions

  • Wait a few minutes—some banks delay updates.
  • Refresh; if still missing, unlink/re-link the connection.
  • Contact us with merchant name, amount, and date.

Hidden balances (••••)

  • Use the balance visibility toggle in the app to show or hide amounts.

6) Subscription & billing

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Open your Apple ID subscriptions on your device to cancel or manage. CleanSlate does not handle billing directly. You can cancel anytime.

When am I charged?

Apple bills on your renewal date as shown in your App Store subscription details.
