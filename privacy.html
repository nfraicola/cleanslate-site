1) Who we are

CleanSlate (“we,” “us,” “our”) provides personal finance tools to help you understand spending and credit card activity.

2) What this policy covers

This policy explains what information we access or process, how we use it, how it’s shared, and your choices. It applies to our iOS app and our website.

3) Financial data via Plaid

When you choose to link a financial account, we use Plaid, Inc. (“Plaid”) to connect to your financial institution. We do not receive or store your banking credentials. Plaid provides us with read-only data such as account identifiers, balances, and transaction details so we can display insights in the app. You can unlink accounts at any time from within CleanSlate or your financial institution’s settings.

4) Information we process

  • Account & transaction data (via Plaid): account name/type, masked identifiers, balances, transactions (amount, date, merchant, category).
  • App data: device info, app version, crash logs, diagnostics.
  • Contact data (if you provide it): email for support, feedback, or account communications.

5) How we use information

  • Provide core features (show spending, categorize transactions, insights).
  • Maintain app functionality, security, and debugging.
  • Communicate service updates and respond to support requests.
  • Comply with law and enforce terms.

6) What we do not do

  • We do not store or see your bank login credentials.
  • We do not sell personal information.
  • We do not enable money movement, payments, or credit decisions.

7) Sharing

  • Vendors/Processors: we use trusted providers (e.g., Plaid for data aggregation, crash/analytics tools) under contracts limiting their use to our instructions.
  • Legal/safety: to comply with applicable law or protect rights, safety, and security.
  • Business transfers: if we undergo a reorganization or transaction, data may transfer under equivalent protections.

8) Data retention

We aim to minimize retention. Financial data is fetched read-only from Plaid. If we temporarily cache data to improve performance, we do so securely and purge it on a rolling basis. Support communications are retained as needed to resolve issues and meet legal obligations.

9) Your choices & rights

  • Unlink accounts: disconnect a financial account in-app or through your bank.
  • Access/Deletion: email us to request a copy of your data or ask us to delete it.
  • Do Not Track/Opt-out: manage analytics/crash sharing in your device settings (where available).

Residents of certain regions (e.g., EEA/UK under GDPR; CA under CCPA/CPRA) may have additional rights including access, correction, deletion, portability, and restriction/objection. We honor these where applicable.

10) Security

We use administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect information (e.g., transport encryption, least-privilege access, vendor due diligence). No system is 100% secure; please use a strong passcode/biometrics on your device.

11) Children

CleanSlate is not directed to children under 13 (or the age required by your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly process children’s personal information.

12) International transfers

If information is processed outside your region, we rely on appropriate safeguards permitted by law.

13) Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. We’ll revise the “Effective date” and, where required, notify you of material changes.

Manually Tracked Cards

If you choose to manually track a card, we store the balance you submit so the app can display totals and projections. This data is not connected to any financial institution or bank credentials.

You can delete your manually tracked card data at any time from within the app, or request a copy of your data by contacting support.
